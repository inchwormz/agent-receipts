# Public reliability data licence

Project-authored records under `public-data/` are licensed under the
[Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International licence](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

The Receipts source code remains licensed under MIT. External datasets are not
redistributed unless their original licence explicitly permits redistribution;
their source, retrieval date, content hash, methodology, attribution, and
licence remain provenance metadata in local imports and calibration bundles.
